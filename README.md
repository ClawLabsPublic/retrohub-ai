# RetroHub

> Play classic games, create AI games, and collect retro bundles.

## Overview
RetroHub AI is a premium online retro gaming portal that brings together browser-based classic game emulation, AI-powered minigame creation, and curated plug-and-play hardware bundles — all in one zero-install platform. Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and React on a Supabase backend, RetroHub AI was developed with AI-assisted development using Claude Code, enabling rapid iteration across its emulation engine, storefront, and generative game features.

## Key Features
- **17-Console Browser Emulator:** Play NES online, N64, PS1, GBA, SNES, Sega Genesis, and more directly in your browser — no downloads, no plugins. Includes CRT filters, savestates, and full gamepad support.
- **AI Arcade:** Powered by Anthropic Claude, the AI Arcade lets you play AI-generated minigames or prompt the system to create your own custom game experiences in seconds.
- **Retro Hardware Bundles:** Shop curated premium Batocera, RetroPie, and Recalbox bundles pre-configured for instant retro gaming setups. Payments are handled securely via Stripe.
- **Cloud Save & Assets:** Game states and assets are stored via AWS S3, so your progress is always available across sessions.
- **Modern UI/UX:** Built with Tailwind CSS and Radix UI for an accessible, responsive interface that feels as polished as the games it hosts.

## How It Works
1. **Browse & Play** — Choose from hundreds of classic games across 17 emulated consoles directly in your browser.
2. **Create with AI** — Use the AI Arcade to generate unique minigames by entering a prompt; Claude handles the game logic and assets in real time.
3. **Shop Bundles** — Select a pre-configured RetroPie or Batocera bundle, check out via Stripe, and receive a ready-to-use retro gaming device.

## FAQ

### Can I play NES and SNES games online without downloading anything?
Yes — RetroHub AI provides a fully browser-based emulator that supports NES, SNES, and 15 other classic consoles with no downloads or plugins required. Just open the site, choose your game, and start playing instantly.

### What is the AI Arcade and how does it generate games?
The AI Arcade uses Anthropic Claude to generate playable minigames based on prompts you provide. You can either play a curated selection of AI-created games or input your own prompt to generate a unique game experience in real time.

### What retro gaming bundles does RetroHub AI sell?
RetroHub AI offers premium pre-configured hardware bundles running Batocera, RetroPie, and Recalbox — popular open-source retro gaming operating systems. Each bundle is plug-and-play, meaning you get a fully set-up retro gaming device delivered to your door.

### Is RetroHub AI a good alternative to setting up RetroPie myself?
Absolutely. Instead of sourcing hardware, flashing an SD card, and configuring RetroPie manually, RetroHub AI bundles ship pre-configured and ready to use. It's ideal for collectors or newcomers who want a premium retro setup without the technical overhead.

### Does RetroHub AI support gamepads and controllers in the browser?
Yes, RetroHub AI includes full gamepad and controller support via the browser emulator, along with CRT scanline filters and savestates so you can enjoy an authentic retro gaming experience on any modern device.

### What technology powers RetroHub AI?
RetroHub AI is built on Next.js, TypeScript, React, and Supabase, with AWS S3 for cloud storage, Stripe for payments, and Anthropic Claude for AI game generation. The platform was developed with AI-assisted tooling via Claude Code, allowing for fast feature development and iteration.
